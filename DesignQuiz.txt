The IMX219-77 and IMX219-160 are both replacement camera modules for the Raspberry Pi. The IMX219-77 has a resolution of 8 megapixels and a 77-degree field of view. It is compatible with Jetson Nano and CM3/3+/4 expansion boards like Raspberry Pi Compute Module IO board, Compute Module IO Board Plus, Compute Module POE Board of Waveshare, and the StereoPi board1. The IMX219-160 has a resolution of 8 megapixels and a 160-degree field of view. It is also compatible with Jetson Nano1.
